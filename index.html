Invaders

by: Michael Yeh

This game was made for my level 2 project at The League of Amazing Programmers.
Click here to download and play!

1. Why I Made This Game

I made this game because its a classic game that not all people know during modern times. It's a simple game and it would be easy to make and fun to play during your free times. I could have made a game that was more fun like a platformer but this is a start.

2. What I Learned

Some feedbacks where to make the movements smoother. In the beginning, when you are moving in one direction, you cannot move in any other direction. I fixed this by adding the movement codes not only in key Pressed but also key Released.

3. Architecture of My Game

This game has 7 different classes, LeagueInvaders, GameObject, GamePanel, ObjectManager, Projectiles, Aliens, and Rocket ship. The first 3 classes are used to monitor the other sprites and run the whole program. Projectiles, Rocket ship, and Aliens are objects that does certain actions when collided with each other. Rocketship has movement functions with the arrow keys.

4. Technical Implementation

I am most proud of the movement and the mechanics of the Rocketship. It took a long time to figure out how to get that smooth movement and when I did it was really relieving. I added the arrow key movement methods to both key pressed and key released and made the movements smoother.

5. Most Challenging Part

The most challenging process of this project was also fixing the mechanic and movement of the Rocketship. In the beginning there was an error where the background is not refreshing when rocket is moving. I fixed it by refreshing background every frame. I also fixed the movement smoothness by adding the arrow key movement methods to both key pressed and key released and made the movements smoother.

6. Future Fixes and Updates

In the future, I would improve the game by adding mini bosses and a final boss to the game. Not just have unlimited enemies spawn. When I kill a number of enemies, the boss will spawn.
